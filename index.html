Chicken Broast

Ingreients Required

  • chiken 1kg medium size portion
  • cooking oil 3 cups /500 ml for deep frying
  • chicken broast masala 1 packet

Steps of cooking

  1. wash chicken and coat each portion evenly with chicken broast masala
  2. in a deep pot heat oil (180 c / 356 f)
  3. fry few pieces at a time for about 10 minutes.turn occasionally.(Reduce the heat if the crust stars to brown too quickly)Repeat for remaining chicken pieces.

Serving suggestion

  • Serve hot with Tamarind Chutney
